Get to know us!

Who are we?

The Eternal Light Free Company on Ragnarok (est. 2018) is a close-knitted family, where having fun and going on adventures together is fundamental and key. Due common activities and events, or just chilling together at one place, it never gets boring! If you are searching for a chill FC with a known circle, then this may be the right place for you!
