Get to know us!

Who are we?

The Eternal Light Free Company (est. 2018) is a close-knitted family that enjoys spending time together with our dearest friends. Without mentioning any of below, our members are aware of simple guidelines to ensure a warm and peaceful atmosphere.
Many cultures mix together in our FC, since our members come from all over the globe. Due that, you have to not worry about inactivity, since you will always find somebody online to play together with.
To make sure this family-feeling maintains and won't be hurt by anyone, our members agree to simply follow our FC guidelines:

Freedom of Speech

We put much worth and importance into the opinions of all our members. Being able to speak out clearly and freely is an important guideline for us to maintain a comfortable stay in our FC. If there are any conveniences happening, or issues between a few members, our Officers will always be ready to help out and support everyone.

Respect and Love

Our FC is like a small melting pot. We have members from different countries, with different beliefs and world view, and different cultures. We love each other for what we are and what we can become, and our support for each other never fades away. May it be misunderstandings or even little fightings, in the end we always remember that we are a family.

Activity and Communication

We all stay in contact and prevent misunderstandings mostly by talking to each other. Being active helps everyone to progress faster, but also enjoy the gameplay together more and more. If there are problems regarding to have no time for gaming anymore, our members can simply tell us. There will be no penalties for not being active, only advantages to enjoy the game more ;)

Frequently Asked Questions

We are a little cozy family called the Eternal Light. You can find our Mansion at the Mist Ward 3, Plot 12. - Everybody is welcome to visit us and hang up with us together!

Since our Leader and Officers are located in Europe, many of the FC events will be timed properly to afternoon times around GMT+1. Either way we still got members from all around the globe, so there's nothing to worry about inactivity.

Our Officers are working on hosting many events in 2 or 3 weeks period between each event. We actively think about unique activities to play together with our members. Besides the events you can find us enjoying all game content together ingame, and if you feel like it, you can also join us in the voice chat on Discord!

The Eternal Light Company is located in the Chaos datacenter - at Ragnarok. Although even people of cross-servers are very welcome to join our conversations and gameplay!

Our FC has one static actively raiding the current Edens Gate savage raids. Their goal is to get BiS in a short time. - We are ready to create an additional static if our members wish for it. This doesn't make us a hardcore or semi-hardcore FC. We've got hardcore raiders, semi hardcore raiders but also many casual players, so any kind of gameplay is possible!
